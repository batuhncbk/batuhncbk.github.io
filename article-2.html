How I start Blender and Youtube ?

After completing my engineering degree, I embarked on the job search process, which turned out to be quite lengthy. It was during this period that I decided to pick up a new hobby. I delved into learning Blender, a 3D design program, and found myself in an entirely different world.

I've come to enjoy this endeavor so much that it has altered my career aspirations. I now identify myself as a 3D artist. Every day, I strive to improve my skills, and I share my progress with you through various videos on my YouTube channel.

I would be thrilled and deeply grateful if you followed my journey by clicking on the link below. Your support means the world to me and brings me immense joy. Thank you!
