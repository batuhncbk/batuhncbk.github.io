Who am I ?


Celebrating the Journey from Mechanical Engineer to Rocketeer and Game Developer Hello! I want to share how I discovered my passions and delved into exciting projects as a graduate mechanical engineer.

I studied mechanical engineering in college and that's when I got introduced to rocketry. Designing rockets, writing software, and understanding their mechanics fascinated me. During this journey, I took my first steps into the 3D modeling world with Blender and later ventured into game development with Unity.

I share my 3D models on YouTube while simultaneously working on the foundations of my game projects using Unity.

I would be thrilled if you could support me on my YouTube channel during this journey!
