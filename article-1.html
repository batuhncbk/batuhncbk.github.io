Who am I as a Mechanical engineer ?

I began my mechanical engineering education by greatly enhancing my English skills in the first year. In the second year, I focused on my studies to deepen my theoretical knowledge. In the following years, I worked with a great passion and determination to improve myself, especially in practical engineering applications. Alongside my team members, I engaged in robotics projects and model rocketry, gaining real-world engineering experience.

I studied mechanical engineering in college and that's when I got introduced to rocketry. Designing rockets, writing software, and understanding their mechanics fascinated me. During this journey, I took my first steps into the 3D modeling world with Blender and later ventured into game development with Unity.

During this time, I actively participated in various engineering projects, including circuit board designs, embedded system software, mechanical system designs, and more. My goal was continuous self-improvement in these diverse fields of engineering. All of these experiences have helped me enhance my engineering skills and problem-solving abilities.

If you'd like to take a closer look at my achievements and projects, you can follow the updates on my LinkedIn profile.
