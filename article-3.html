How I met with Unity Engine ?

In my 3D design journey that began with the Blender program, a thought started to form: that my designs come to life in a real world and, combined with compelling stories, create immersive gaming experiences. This idea led me to explore the Unity game engine and start building my own template.

I've come to enjoy this endeavor so much that it has altered my career aspirations. I now identify myself as a 3D artist. Every day, I strive to improve my skills, and I share my progress with you through various videos on my YouTube channel.

While working on creating a template, I continue to enhance my skills and lose myself in the Unity game engine. When I reach a certain skill level, I will present to you wonderful stories I'll enjoy telling and cinematic scenes that will truly transport players into the worlds I've created.

I can say that I'm on the path to dedicating my life to this art form. Currently, on my page, you can see that what I've created within Unity is not complete games yet but rather a demo. I invite you to explore my work through the link below."
